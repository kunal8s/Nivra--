About Nivra

Our mission to transform domestic work

Nivra is a dignity-first platform connecting domestic workers with households through a fair, transparent subscription model. We believe in creating equitable opportunities and fostering trust between service providers and recipients.

Founded in 2023, we're on a mission to revolutionize the domestic work industry by putting workers' rights and fair treatment at the center of everything we do.
